# Module: Alternatives

**Depends on:** Macro regime (USD, China demand, inflation), Rate environment
**Feeds into:** Cross-asset (diversification, correlation), Thematic (commodity-linked themes)

*Activate for commodities, crypto, REITs, or infrastructure.*

---

## Commodities

- **Supply/demand balances:** Inventory data, OPEC decisions, mine supply, crop reports
- **Carry and roll yield:** Contango vs backwardation — what does the curve shape tell you?
- **Macro linkages:** USD correlation, China demand proxy, inflation hedge characteristics
- **Positioning:** Managed money net longs/shorts (CFTC)
- **Seasonal patterns:** Where relevant

---

## Crypto

- **On-chain metrics:** Active addresses, exchange flows, whale activity (where available)
- **Regulatory developments:** Material regulatory changes or proposals
- **Institutional adoption:** ETF flows, corporate treasury holdings, custody infrastructure
- **Correlation regime:** Trading as risk-on beta, digital gold, or idiosyncratic?
- **Supply dynamics:** Halving cycle positioning, staking yields, protocol-specific catalysts

---

## REITs / Listed Infrastructure

- **NAV discount/premium** to underlying assets
- **Cap rate spreads** vs bond yields
- **Occupancy rates and rental growth**
- **Interest rate sensitivity**
- **Sub-sector dynamics:** Data centres, logistics, healthcare, towers
