# Module: Fixed Income

**Depends on:** Macro regime (inflation, central bank stance), Credit conditions
**Feeds into:** Cross-asset (equity risk premium, relative value vs equities), FX (rate differentials)

*Activate when the report involves bonds, rates, credit, or yield curve analysis.*

---

## Always Address

- **Rate level and direction:** Benchmark yields (10Y UST, Bund, Gilt, JGB) vs fair value models (nominal GDP trend, Taylor rule, term premium models). What is priced into OIS/futures?
- **Yield curve:** Shape (steep, flat, inverted), curvature, historical signal. Term premium — compressed or elevated? Steepener vs flattener dynamics.
- **Credit spreads:** IG and HY spreads vs history and fundamentals. Spread per unit of leverage. Default cycle positioning. Distress ratios.
- **Duration positioning:** Given cycle, inflation, and central bank outlook — long or short duration? Convexity considerations.
- **Supply/demand technicals:** Issuance calendar, QT pace, foreign demand, corporate refinancing wall.
- **Real yields:** TIPS breakevens, real rate levels — attractive for long-term allocators?

---

## Conditional Depth Triggers

| Sub-topic | Trigger | Additional Questions |
|---|---|---|
| **Sovereign rates** | Government bonds focus | Fiscal trajectory, debt sustainability, auction demand, safe-haven dynamics, forward guidance parsing |
| **Credit (IG/HY)** | Corporate credit focus | Leverage trends, interest coverage, rating migration, fallen angel risk, sector dispersion, covenant quality |
| **EM debt** | Emerging market fixed income | Local vs hard currency, real rate differentials, FX carry, political risk, IMF programme risk, commodity linkages |
| **Inflation-linked** | TIPS or linkers | Breakeven analysis, inflation swap pricing, seasonal patterns, energy pass-through to CPI |
