# Module: Derivatives & Risk Management

**Depends on:** Volatility environment, asset-specific modules (for hedge sizing)
**Feeds into:** Actionable Conclusions (hedge recommendations), Scenarios (tail risk)

*Activate when the report involves hedging, tail risk, or optionality strategies.*

---

## Core Framework

- **Volatility analysis:** Implied vs realised vol, vol term structure (contango/backwardation), smile/skew
- **Hedging strategies:** Protective puts, collars, put spreads — cost-effectiveness at current vol levels
- **Tail risk:** Fat-tail scenarios and how to hedge. Cost of tail protection (OTM put pricing).
- **Cross-asset hedges:** Using one asset to hedge another (e.g., long JPY as equity tail hedge, long gold as inflation/geopolitical hedge)
- **Carry and roll:** Cost of maintaining hedges over the investment horizon
- **Greeks analysis:** Key sensitivities for any recommended options positions

---

## Working Brief Update

Note vol regime, hedge cost assessment, and any asymmetric risk/reward setups identified.
