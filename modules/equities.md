# Module: Equities

**Depends on:** Macro regime (rate environment, cycle position), Thematic findings (if theme-driven)
**Feeds into:** Cross-asset (relative valuation vs bonds/alternatives), Scenarios (return estimates)

*Activate when the report involves listed equity markets, sectors, regions, or styles.*

---

## Always Address

- **Valuation:** Forward P/E, CAPE/Shiller P/E, earnings yield vs bond yields (equity risk premium), P/B, EV/EBITDA as relevant. Percentile rank vs own history. Relative to other regions/sectors. Always specify time periods for any valuation change.
- **Earnings cycle:** Revision cycle position — upgrades or downgrades? Growth trajectory. Margin trends (input costs, pricing power, labour). Key earnings themes from recent reporting.
- **Style & factor positioning:** Growth vs value — which is favoured by current macro? Small vs large cap — credit conditions and cyclical implications. Quality and momentum signals.
- **Sector dynamics:** Which sectors benefit from the current cycle phase? Rotation signals. Relative valuations across sectors.
- **Flows & sentiment:** Fund flows (active vs passive, regional shifts). Positioning (hedge fund net exposure, retail sentiment). Put/call ratios, VIX term structure.
- **Technical context:** Key support/resistance, trend strength, breadth (advance/decline, % above 200DMA).

---

## Conditional Depth Triggers

Activate these sub-analyses when specifically relevant:

| Sub-topic | Trigger | Additional Questions |
|---|---|---|
| **Regional comparison** | User mentions regions or allocation | Relative valuation gap (historical percentile), earnings growth differentials, currency impact, political risk premium, sector composition bias, capital flow trends |
| **Growth vs Value** | User mentions style or factor tilts | Yield curve slope implications, growth outperformance duration, rate sensitivity, quality screens within each, earnings delivery rates |
| **Small vs Large cap** | User mentions market cap | Credit conditions impact, M&A cycle, IPO pipeline, liquidity premium, earnings breadth across cap spectrum |
| **Single sector** | User names a sector | Supply/demand fundamentals, regulatory landscape, capex cycle, competitive dynamics, key company signals |
| **Single country / EM** | User names a country | Sovereign risk (CDS, ratings), current account, FX reserves, political calendar, local monetary policy, foreign ownership levels |
| **Thematic equity** | Theme touching equities | Defer to Thematic module for framework — this module provides equity-specific valuation and positioning context |

---

## "So What?" Requirement

Every subsection must close with a positioning implication. *"European banks trade at 0.8x book, a 30% discount to 10-year average — the valuation case is compelling"* is incomplete. Add: *"...which supports an overweight in European financials vs US financials, particularly if ECB cuts are slower than priced."*
